fix(reader): group chapter navigation — treat sub-chapters as pages within their parent chapter

Bug 1: 向下滑动/翻子页面时，章节数不应一起增加
Bug 2: 有子页面的章节，向右翻页实应进入组内子页面而非跳到下一章

Root cause: `_flatChapters` flattened parent+child chapters into a single list,
and `_currentChapterIndex` indexed into it directly. Every entry was treated as
a separate "chapter", so navigating to a sub-chapter incremented the chapter
index.

Fix:
- Introduce `_ChapterGroup` — one top-level chapter + its sub-children form a group.
- Track `_currentGroupIndex` (which group/top-level chapter) and
  `_currentSubPageIndex` (which page within the group).
- Navigation: advance through sub-pages within the group first; only cross
  groups when the entire group is exhausted.
- Preserve backward compatibility: saved flat `chapterIndex` is restored via
  `_restorePositionFromFlatIndex()`.
