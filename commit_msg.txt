fix(reader): image-load re-pagination, sub-page display, and spine validation

Three targeted fixes for page numbering accuracy:

1. Image-load re-pagination (reader_content.dart)
   - Track _lastMaxScrollExtent and detect content height changes
   - Schedule re-checks at 200/500/1200/2500ms after each content load
   - Auto-recalculate _totalPages when images finish loading and expand layout

2. Sub-page display in navigation bar (reader_navigation_bar.dart)
   - Add optional subPageIndex/subPageCount parameters
   - Show "子页 X/Y · 第 A/B 页" when chapter has sub-pages
   - Show plain "第 A/B 页" when no sub-pages exist

3. Spine order validation logging (epub_parser_service.dart)
   - Print complete Spine entry list with Anchor + Title at parse time
   - Enables comparison with Calibre/Sigil to detect ordering mismatches
